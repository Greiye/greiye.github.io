Mounts Rent Rate:
Example: A draft horse would cost 25 gold deposit up front (50% of the purchase price). The vendor would then take 1 gold from your deposit for every day you rent it (2% of the purchase price). Upon return, the vendor would refund you your unused deposit, or you would pay any amount owed over the deposit price.
